About Our WebSite

AI Uniform is Pakistan’s first and largest online uniform store providing the best quality school uniform, school accessories and customer service experience to thousands of valued consumers in Pakistan. AI Uniform is not only an online store, it’s a Joint Venture of different uniform manufacturers across Pakistan. Our Aim is to select best quality stuff and make it available to our customers at their doorsteps. Uniform.pk wanted to redefine the online consumer experience by following international quality standards.Initially our target audience are, who wanted to purchase the best quality school uniform and accessories at their doorsteps in affordable prices, Uniform.pk has started to branch out to other categories in order to cater to other audiences. In the future, you will be able to view several different products belonging to schools on our website.

Our Mission

AI Uniform mission is to help local consumers make confident purchasing decisions at their doorsteps, when they don’t know exactly where to buy.

Being managed by a small and dedicated team of young individuals, we put every bit of our love, dedication and commitment in delivering every order. We want to thank all of our customers for support & motivation.
